🛡️ Safety Tips

Essential rules to protect yourself from digital scams in India.

🔐

Never share OTPNo bank or app will ever ask for your OTP, PIN, or CVV.

🔗

Check URLsLook for typos like "sbi-bank.com" or extra words in real domain names.

💳

UPI = Only outgoingScanning a QR code or entering a PIN sends money — never receives.

📞

Verify callersHang up and call back using the official number from the website.

📱

Avoid APKsNever install apps from WhatsApp, SMS, or unknown websites.

🏦

Helpline: 1930Call India's cybercrime helpline immediately if you're scammed.

⚡

Urgency = Red flagScammers always create fake urgency. Take time to verify first.

🎁

Free = ScamNo one gives free money. If it sounds too good, it's a trap.
