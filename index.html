Welcome ScamY

Fake websites look real until your money disappears faster than your common sense.

OTP Scams

Scammers trick you into sharing your one-time password by pretending to be bank or service staff. Once you share it, they use it to access your account and steal money. One-time password becomes one-time regret.

Call Forward Scams

Fraudsters ask you to dial a code or forward calls for "verification." This redirects your calls to them, allowing them to receive OTPs and control your banking access. Your calls get redirected, your money follows.

Fake Message of Bank

You receive urgent SMS or WhatsApp messages pretending to be from your bank. They include fake links or numbers to steal your login details or money. Looks urgent. Is fake. Ends expensive.

Fake Call

Scammers call pretending to offer prizes, jobs, or bank help. They pressure you to share details or make payments, leading to financial loss. You didn't win. You just got selected to lose money.

Safety Tips

Do's

  • Verify unknown calls before sharing info
  • Use strong passwords 🔑
  • Enable 2FA security
  • Check website URL carefully
  • Install apps from trusted sources only 📱
  • Keep your apps updated

Don'ts

  • Never share OTP, PIN, CVV
  • Don't click unknown links 🚫
  • Don't trust fake prize messages
  • Don't install APK from unknown sites
  • Don't share screen with strangers
