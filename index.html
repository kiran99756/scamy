Know the Scam Types
Recognising patterns is your first line of defence.
📲

OTP Scams

Scammers trick you into sharing your one-time password by pretending to be bank staff. Once you share it, they access your account instantly. One-time password becomes one-time regret.

📞

Call Forward Scams

Fraudsters ask you to dial a code for "verification." This redirects your calls to them, allowing them to intercept OTPs and drain your account. Your calls get hijacked, your money follows.

💬

Fake Bank Messages

Urgent SMS or WhatsApp messages from fake bank numbers with phishing links. Looks urgent. Is fake. Ends expensive.

🎭

Fake CBI/Police Calls

Scammers impersonate CBI, police, or TRAI officials to extort money through fabricated legal threats. You didn't commit a crime — they did.

Safety Tips
Simple rules that protect you every day.
✅ Do's
✓
Verify unknown calls before sharing any information
✓
Use strong, unique passwords for banking apps 🔑
✓
Enable 2-factor authentication on all accounts
✓
Check website URLs carefully before entering details
✓
Install apps only from official Google Play or App Store 📱
✓
Keep all apps and OS updated regularly
❌ Don'ts
✕
Never share OTP, PIN, CVV with anyone — ever
✕
Don't click links from unknown SMS/WhatsApp numbers 🚫
✕
Don't trust surprise prize or lottery messages
✕
Don't install APK files from unknown websites
✕
Don't share screen with unknown callers or apps
