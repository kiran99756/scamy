Real Digital Scams

Learn from real-life cases to protect yourself from online frauds in India.

Digital Arrest Scam – Naresh Malhotra

Scammers posed as CBI officials and held the victim on video calls for 47 days, stealing ₹22.92 crore.

Fake Police Scam – Delhi

Victim was coerced by scammers impersonating police, losing ₹2.19 crore in the process.

Retired Professor Scam – Madhya Pradesh

A retired professor was accused of terrorism links online, losing about ₹10 lakh under threat.

UPI Hijack Scam – Delhi

Cyber criminals hijacked phones and used Aadhaar info to manipulate UPI transactions in 100+ cases.

Aadhaar Digital Arrest – Haridwar

Thieves used Aadhaar to threaten legal action and stole ₹25 lakh from an elderly victim.

Crypto Scam – ₹2,200 Crore Network

Fraudulent apps like HPZ tokens defrauded users of massive cryptocurrency investments across India.

Voice Phishing Scam – Anjani Kumar Srivastava

Scammers mimicked a friend's voice to trick the 72-year-old into transferring ₹81,747 via UPI.

WhatsApp APK Scam

Victim installed a fake SBI app via WhatsApp and lost ₹22,000 in unauthorized transactions.

Courier UPI Scam

Scammers pretending to be a courier service forced the victim to pay ₹2,898 using WhatsApp Pay.

UPI Unauthorized Debits – Delhi

A user lost ₹51,000 in multiple unauthorized UPI transactions even without sharing OTP.
